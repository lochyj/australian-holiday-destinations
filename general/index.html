Description

This graph shows the number of countries each of the respondents had travelled to. 56% of respondents have travelled to less than 5 countries, while only 15% had travelled to more than 10 countries.

Respondent demographics

These graphs show the demographics of the respondents of the survey, these demographics may not be 100% reflective of the entirety of Australia due to the small sample size and limited community. However these do allow us to have an even distribution of demographics
