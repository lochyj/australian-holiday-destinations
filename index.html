Holiday Destinations of Australians



About Project

We chose to investigate why, where and how often JMSS students travel. This was due to the complex nature of choosing holiday destinations and we decided we wanted to know more about the factors that influence such decisions. Furthermore, We wanted to compare and contrast the destinations of JMSS students to the rest of australia to help people choose a suitable holiday destination.
