Travel habits of different ethnicities

This shows the different travel locations of people of different ethnicity's. The majority of Asian respondents have traveled to Asia, this may be due to family or cultural ties to the continent, while White respondents have traveled to a wider variety of continents. This could be due to traveling for solely tourism rather than other options
