Secondary Data

Countries that australians travel to

This data from the ABS shows that the common destinations of Australians, similar to the states data, we can see that a lot of people are travelling to other nearby countries in Oceania, likely due to the proximity of these countries.

States that Australians travel tot

This has the interstate travel destinations of all Australians, so the data can be treated differently to the data from our survey, as our survey only included people from Victoria, however, we can see that the most common destinations include New South Whales, Queensland and Victoria. Similar to our collected data, this is likely due to the landmarks at these locations that are popular amongst tourists
