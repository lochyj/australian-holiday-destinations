Secondary Data

* The maps below represent a collation of data from our survey. Each country / state will be highlighted with a colour if someone has / wants to visit that country. The darker the country, the greater the number of people that has / wants to travel there.


Countries that australians travel to

This data from the ABS shows that the common destinations of Australians, similar to the states data, we can see that a lot of people are traveling to other nearby countries in Oceania, likely due to the proximity of these countries.

Data courtesy: (ABS, 2023)

Common interstate destinations

This has the interstate travel destinations of all Australians, so the data can be treated differently to the data from our survey, as our survey only included people from Victoria, however, we can see that the most common destinations include New South Whales, Queensland and Victoria. Similar to our collected data, this is likely due to the proximity of these states to the population centres and the cost of traveling to far away states such as the NT and WA (USYD, 2021).

Data courtesy: (ABS, 2023)
